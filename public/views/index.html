Estimate your net salary

When you are thinking about getting a job in another country, it is important to know the personal income tax rate in that country and the net salary you would take home. In certain countries tax rates on salaries can be up to 50% of the gross amount. This simple service helps you to estimate your net salary in various countries, so that you can be more confident that the salary proposed in a job contract would be enough for the quality of life you're seeking in the country of your choice.
